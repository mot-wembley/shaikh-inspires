Gazal Shaikh just inspired everyone in Qatar with her creation

Gazal Shaikh is a  Qatar based Fashion and  Lifestyle blogger featuring all the best events happening in Qatar. She is popular due to its quality content and honest reviews. Also, she is an immensely talented fashion designer based in Qatar. Through her creative aspirations, IAMGAZAL in 2014 was born.

Through her blog, IAMGAZAL, she continues to innovate and inspire others by sharing her thoughts and views so that they feel good about themselves. Efforts to promote, inform and empower all women around the globe through fashion tips, fashion trends, personal style, product reviews, lifestyle, beauty secrets.  That led her to partner with brands such as Rolls Royce, Victoria Secret, Massimo Dutti, Versace, Master Card, Hyundai, Swissline Skincare, The Pearl Qatar (UDC), West Elm, to name a few.

She is dedicated to quality over quantity, which is why Gazal is very careful when working with any brand and does her research before supporting anything on her blog by testing the quality and authenticity of the product and company.

Gazal’s main focus is not just on promoting fashion and lifestyle through her blog, she wants people to be better versions of themselves, and that’s why she updates her knowledge and experience on a timely basis. One should not depend on mere materialistic needs, but family and self-growth are important. IAMGAZAL is growing its success over time not only for its quality content and credible feedback but also for its frank and forward-looking statements. She believes that human beings should be treated fairly wherever they come from.

Each article she creates is a creative musical, arranged for the public as well as classes delivering the best and the latest in fashion through partnerships with the best in the industry. Satisfying her creative drive by producing quality creations and sharing those with love with her viewers across the world.
